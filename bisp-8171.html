ℹ️ What is the BISP Kafaalat Programme 2026?

The Benazir Income Support Programme (BISP) is Pakistan's flagship poverty alleviation initiative, providing unconditional cash transfers to the most vulnerable households. The primary Benazir Kafaalat stipend has been raised to Rs. 14,500 per quarter for 2026 to help families combat rising inflation and utility costs.

🎓 BISP Taleemi Wazaif: Education Stipend 2026

Taleemi Wazaif encourages school attendance for children of BISP beneficiaries. Children must maintain a minimum 70% school attendance. Female students receive a higher stipend to promote girls' education.

📋 NSER Dynamic Registry — New Registration

In 2026, BISP has transitioned from the old static NSER survey to the BISP Dynamic Registry. If your 8171 status shows "Na-Ahal" (Ineligible), or your financial situation has recently worsened, you can now visit any of the 600+ BISP Tehsil offices to update your poverty score.

Required Documents for NSER Survey

  • Original CNIC of the female head of household
  • Updated B-Form (NADRA) for all children in the family
  • A registered mobile SIM card in the applicant's name
  • Latest electricity or gas bill (if available)
🚨

Fraud Alert — Scam Prevention

BISP communicates only through shortcode 8171. If you receive a WhatsApp message, a call from a regular mobile number, or any SMS from a code other than 8171 claiming Ehsaas funds — it is a scam. Never share your CNIC, ATM PIN, or bank details.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Is the Ehsaas Program 25000 still active in 2026?+
No. The Ehsaas 25,000 cash grant was an emergency relief program for floods and COVID-19. The permanent ongoing programme is BISP Kafaalat, distributing Rs. 14,500 quarterly.
Why did my BISP payment stop?+
Payments stop if your household status improves — e.g., someone gets a government job, travels abroad, or buys a vehicle — or if NADRA records are not updated. Visit a Dynamic Registry desk at your local BISP Tehsil office to recertify.
What is the BISP Nashonuma Programme?+
Nashonuma provides cash stipends and nutritious food to pregnant women, lactating mothers, and children under two to prevent malnutrition and stunting.
Can men apply for BISP Kafaalat?+
No. The stipend is issued strictly to the female head of the eligible household to ensure financial empowerment of women and proper use of funds.
