What is Tools2Do? Your Guide to Digital Pakistan

In an era of rapid digitization, the Government of Pakistan has shifted numerous citizen services, social safety nets, and educational portals online. However, navigating these scattered official websites can be overwhelming. Tools2Do.com bridges this gap by acting as an independent, centralized digital utility hub.

Whether you need to generate a duplicate HAZECO or PESCO Electricity Bill, or check the BISP 8171 Web Portal for the latest Benazir Kafaalat stipends, our platform provides streamlined, verified tools and direct links to official resources.

Why Trust Our Information?

We focus on semantic clarity, fast load times, and superior user experience. Our guides are updated regularly to reflect the latest policies, such as the 2026 shift to the BISP Dynamic Registry, and the exact eligibility requirements for targeted fuel subsidies via 9771. By breaking down complex bureaucratic processes into simple, bilingual (English and Urdu) step-by-step guides, we ensure that every citizen can access their utilities and benefits without hassle.
